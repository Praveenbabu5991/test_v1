DTSE CZ TRAINING FEEDBACK FROM ANALYSIS


Aim of the tool

The Training Feedback Form includes quantitative and open text questions.The Form analysis tool classifies Open Text Feedback into three categories(Positive,Neutral,Negative) without you having to check manually every feedback

Steps

  • Download the Feedback form as a Microsoft Excell file(filename.xlsc)
  • Upload the file through the form on the right export it

Output

  • ALl quantitative data remains on the Sheet1!
  • The Text Feedback and Category are added to the Excell file in "sheet2"
